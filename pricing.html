Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DEIVERSE pricing

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation. After that, it's securely deleted according to our data retention policy.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team for a full refund.

Is there a free trial for Pro plans?

Yes, Pro plans include a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.
